Summary

 

As a Lead Software Engineer on the Backend Services team, you will take ownership of core API services and contracts within Paramount Streaming’s content discovery and engagement systems. You will drive API design, contribute to system architecture, and partner closely with peers, product managers, and stakeholders to deliver reliable, scalable, and high-performance APIs for Paramount+ and Pluto TV. This role is focused on leading API development for a next-generation streaming personalization engine, a real-time inference platform that dynamically determines the optimal streaming experiences for each viewer.

You will design and build APIs that:

Expose real-time decisioning and personalization capabilities
Integrate high-volume user event data and content metadata systems
Enable seamless communication between content systems, video and ad services, and client applications
This role requires both deep technical expertise and the ability to lead engineers through influence, shaping platform direction and enabling engineering velocity at scale.

 

Responsibilities

 

This is a meaningful role with a wide range of responsibilities, including:

Design, build, and maintain high-performance, scalable APIs that power content, personalization, and engagement experiences
Define and enforce API standards and best practices, including versioning, contract design, and backward compatibility
Lead the development of APIs supporting personalization engines, enabling real-time decisioning and low-latency responses
Collaborate with frontend, backend, data, and platform teams to ensure clean, reliable system integrations
Evolve existing content management and metadata APIs to support dynamic, personalized experiences
Design APIs for event-driven systems, integrating with Kafka, Pub/Sub, or similar messaging platforms
Optimize APIs for performance, scalability, and reliability under high traffic conditions
Leverage AI-assisted development tools to accelerate API development, testing, and debugging
Contribute to CI/CD pipelines, automated testing, and release processes to ensure safe and efficient deployments
Participate in architecture discussions, RFCs, and cross-team technical initiatives
Mentor engineers and promote best practices in API design and system development
